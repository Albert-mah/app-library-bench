What is our refund window for enterprise annual plans?
94% confidence

Enterprise annual plans include a 30-day full-refund window measured from the original invoice date. Requests inside this window are processed at 100%, typically settling within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.1

After day 30 the contract converts to a non-refundable annual commitment, but customers may request a pro-rated service credit toward a future renewal if they downgrade — this requires approval from the Account Manager.2

  • Eligibility — annual term, paid by invoice or card, no prior refund on the same account
  • Window — 30 calendar days from invoice date 1
  • Exceptions — usage-based overages are never refundable 3
