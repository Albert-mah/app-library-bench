Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Overview

Atlas offers a flexible paid time off program covering vacation, personal days, and sick leave. PTO accrues per pay period and is available to all full-time employees from their first day. This policy applies to all global entities; regional addenda are linked at the bottom.

Quick answer: Full-time employees accrue 1.67 days of PTO per month (20 days / year). Up to 5 unused days carry over into the next calendar year.

Accrual & carryover

Accrual begins on your start date and is granted at the end of each monthly pay period. Carryover is capped to keep balances healthy and encourage rest.

TenureAnnual PTOMonthly accrualCarryover cap
0–2 years20 days1.67 days5 days
3–5 years25 days2.08 days7 days
6+ years28 days2.33 days10 days

How to request time off

All time off is requested and approved in Workday. Submit at least the notice period below so your manager and team can plan coverage.

  • 1–2 days — submit at least 3 business days in advance.
  • 3+ consecutive days — submit at least 2 weeks in advance.
  • Sick leave — notify your manager as early as possible; no advance approval required.
Note: Blackout windows apply during quarter-end close (last week of Mar / Jun / Sep / Dec) for Finance and RevOps teams.

Regional addenda

Local statutory minimums always override this policy where they are more generous. See the EMEA and APAC addenda for country-specific public holidays and sick-pay rules.

  • EMEA — statutory minimum 25 days; UK adds 8 bank holidays.
  • APAC — Singapore 14 days, Japan grants increase with tenure.
